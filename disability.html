Disability Insurance

Disability Insurance provides financial support in the form of monthly payments if you are unable to work due to a serious injury or illness. It ensures you continue to receive an income while you recover.

Key Features of Disability Insurance

Income Replacement: Pays a percentage of your salary during periods of disability.

Coverage Options: Short-term and long-term coverage available depending on your needs.

Flexible Payout: Funds can be used for any expenses, from daily living costs to medical bills.

Customizable Plans: Choose a policy that matches your job, income, and health condition.


Benefits of Disability Insurance

Financial Security: Protects your income and helps cover medical costs, rehabilitation, and more.

Stress Reduction: Focus on recovery without worrying about income loss.

Family Support: Ensures your loved ones are taken care of, even when you’re unable to work.

Protection for All Professions: Tailored coverage for various occupations, from high-risk jobs to office work.

Note

This is a general overview of Disability Insurance. Coverage and benefits can vary depending on the provider, plan, and personal factors like age, health, and occupation. For detailed information, consult a trusted insurance advisor.
